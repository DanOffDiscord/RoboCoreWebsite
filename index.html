What is Robo Core?

Robo Core is a community of developers and gamers of the like that come together to share their ideas and teach others knowledge and show them their skills. We provide you with a friendly community, and tools to help build, modify and expand your creation. Robo Core also has many useful programming tutorials uploaded across the internet to aid you in your path to developing your own project. We at Robo Core allow you to upload your Discord Bots and Servers to our website so that you can allow others to use your creation.
